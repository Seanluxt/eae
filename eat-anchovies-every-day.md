Eat anchovies every day: not the tiny fish, but things that are different, metaphorical anchovies.

This is code for 'Try Things.'

Not just any things but different things.

Things that might be easy to dismiss because they are ‘weird’. Things that most people don't try. Things that hardly anyone will be putting on a plate in front of you and asking you to try. Things that on their face are easy to say no to.

And, related to this, anytime you find someone who is asking you to "Try an anchovy" (either a straight-up tiny fish or just something that is different), try it.

And, take a moment and look at that person closely because maybe that person is an anchovy, a bit different and worth trying.
