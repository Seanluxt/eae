Find happiness in the small everyday things that are all around you and you will find much happiness.

Unless you took a second to look you would never know, but bees are truly miraculous creatures, and their community (hive) is fascinating.

Small things to appreciate as you build your small-things-appreciation-muscle:
1) the simplicity and history and heft of cast iron pans.
2) when no-one is home you can turn the stereo up LOUD!
3) the smell of a lemon - gently push your thumbnail into the skin of a lemon, and then smell the lemonness of the lemon, and your thumb smells like lemon too for a few hours.

And look for other ‘lemons’ that you can borrow from and appreciate.
